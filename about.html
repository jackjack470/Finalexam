Life Before Music

Zavian Flowers (TrapLordVegeta) was born in Atlanta, Georgia, the youngest of seven children. Flowers was initially raised in Forest Park on Southside Atlanta's Conley Road. After dropping out of high school during his freshman year, Flowers moved to Cleveland Avenue. Like his brother and fellow rapper Lil Keed, who is one year older, Gotit did not start taking rap seriously until his friend Scoot was killed a few days before the release of the group compilation mixtape Young Winning Season in 2016. TrapLord then began releasing songs with his brother Lil Keed.

Life After Music

TrapLord has collaborated with Keed and Sleepy Rose for the song "Heavy Metal". In November 2018, he worked with Sleepy Rose again to release the song "Hercules". He has also released the song "Superstar" with L3. TralLord released his debut studio album Recuperation, consisting of 18 singles including "BNB" and "Space Coupe".The album was released on November 16, 2018. The album featured singles including "Blue Slimes" featuring Sleepy Rose, L3 and Keed as well as "Drip Severe". On March 14, 2019 Lil Gotit released his second studio album, Crazy But It's True. On March 29, 2019, Gotit released a music video for his single "Drop The Top" featuring Keed. On April 11, 2019, TrapLord released his single "Never Met" with a music video. On May 2, 2019, TrapLord released his single "Lil Ralph" accompanied with a music video. On May 8, 2019, Famous Dex released an audio for a new song "Fully Loaded" featuring TrapLordVegeta. A music video for Fully Loaded was released later on May 29, 2019. On the same day animation video of TrapLord's single "Da Real HoodBabies (Remix)" featuring Lil Hef was also released. On July 1, 2019, TrapLord released his single "Pop My Shit", the song was accompanied with a music video, along with a remix featuring Keed that later released on the 20th of August. On August 7, 2019 TrapLord released his single "Oh Ok" accompanied with a music video.
